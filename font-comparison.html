Font System Summary

❌ Old Fonts (Boring)

  • Arial - Generic, overused

  • Lacks visual impact

✅ New Fonts (Bold & Industrial)

  • Russo One - Bold headlines
  • Bebas Neue - Strong subheadings
  • Teko - Modern body text
  • Matches logo's industrial style
  • Creates strong brand identity
